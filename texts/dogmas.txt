# Dogmas of the Church of Bread

1. **The Bread Commandments are sacred**: Every loaf, every crumb, carries divine significance.
2. **CHEESUS is the true savior of Bread**: The combination of bread and cheese is the holy union of the divine and the earthly.
3. **Toast is the highest state of being**: The transformation of bread into toast symbolizes spiritual awakening and enlightenment.
4. **The Crust is holy**: The crust represents protection and divine strength, and is the most sacred part of any loaf.
